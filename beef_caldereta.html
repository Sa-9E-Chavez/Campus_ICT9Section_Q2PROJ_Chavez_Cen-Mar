Beef Caldereta

Ingridients:
2 lbs. beef (cubed)
3 pcs. garlic cloves (crushed and chopped)
1 pc. onion (finely chopped)
2 cups beef broth
1 pc. red bell pepper (sliced)
1 pc. green bell pepper (sliced)
1 cup tomato sauce
1/2 cup liver spread (processed using blender)
1 tsp. chili flakes
3 pcs. dried bay leaves
2 cups potatoes (sliced)
2 cups carrots (sliced)
1/4 cup cooking oil
2/3 cup green olives
salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps:


  1. Heat the cooking oil in the cooking pot or pressure cooker.

  2. Sauté the onion and garlic.

  3. Add the beef, then cook for 5 minutes or until the color turns light brown.

  4. Add the dried bay leaves and chili flakes or crushed pepper, stir.

  5. Add the liver spread, then stir.

  6. Pour-in the tomato sauce and beef broth.

  7. Cook the beef until it becomes tender (about 30 mins if using a pressure cooker, or 1 to 2 hours if using an ordinary pot).

  8. Add potato and carrots, then cook for 8 to 10 minutes.

  9. Put the green olives and bell peppers in the cooking pot. Stir and continue to cook for 5 minutes more.

  10. Add salt and pepper to taste.

  11. Serve Hot, enjoy!
