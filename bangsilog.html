Bangsilog

Ingridients:
2 cups of chilled white rice
3 tbsp. minced garlic
1/2 tsp. salt
1 baby boneless bangus (milkfish)
1 egg, cooked sunny side up
cooking oil

Marinade:
3 cloves of garlic, crushed then peeled
4 tbsp. lemon juice
1/4 cups of soy sauce
1/4 tsp. ground pepper

Steps:


    • Milkfish
  1. Marinate the milkfish before frying.

  2. In a large non-stick pan, heat a small amount of cooking oil over high heat.

  3. When the oil gets hot, carefully place the milkfish into the pan with meat side facing down in the oil.

  4. Lower the heat to medium-high then continue to cook the milkfish until it becomes crispy and golden brown.

  5. When it's done. carefully remove the fish from the hot oil and set it on some papertowels to absorb the excess oil.

    • Garlic Fried Rice

  1. Heat a clean frying pan over medium heat.

  2. Add 1 tablespoon of cooking oil followed by 2 tablespoons of minced garlic.

  3. Cook the garlic until it begins to turn golden brown.

  4. Now add the rice to the pan then press it down into the oil and garlic.

  5. Stir and mix the rice continuously to avoid scorching.

  6. Cook the rice for about 5-7 minutes or until the rice is hot and well blended.

  7. Add a little salt to taste then continue to cook for an additional 5 minutes.

  8. Transfer the garlic fried rice to a plate along with the cooked milk fish and fried egg.

  9. Prepare some freshly sliced tomatoes as a side dish if you prefer.

  10. Enjoy!
