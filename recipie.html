Breakfast

Hotsilog

  • A meal composed of hotdogs, garlic fried rice, and fried egg.

BangSilog

  • A meal composed of bangus (milkfish), garlic fried rice, and fried egg.

Tapsilog

  • A meal composed of tapa, garlic fried rice, and fried egg.

Lunch/Dinner

Beef Caldereta

  • A type of beef stew cooked with tomato sauce and liver spread.

Pork Nilaga

  • A type of Filipino pork soup with ingredients such as potato, and saba banana.

Pork Adobo

  • A pork cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.

Dessert

Halo-Halo

  • A popular cold dessert made up of crushed ice, evaporated milk, etc.

Leche Flan

  • A Filipino dessert made with egg yolks, milk, sugar, and vanilla extract.

Coffee Jelly

  • a jelly dessert flavored with coffee and sugar.
